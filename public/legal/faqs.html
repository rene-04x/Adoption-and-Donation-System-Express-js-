FAQ

Your guide to the PAWSsion web-based adoption and donation management system. We aim to provide a transparent, efficient, and data-driven experience for our community.

Adoption Process

How does the Matchmaking System work?

The system uses Rule-Based Weighted Similarity Scoring and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze your preferences. It evaluates criteria such as the type of animal, sex, color, behavior, and age to generate compatibility rankings and recommend the best pets for your lifestyle.

What are the requirements to adopt?

You must complete the Digital Adoption Application Form through the web platform. While the system streamlines screening, final approval still depends on offline procedures such as interviews, background checks, and home visits.

How long does the process take?

The system is designed to improve operational efficiency and provide real-time status tracking of your application. You can monitor your progress directly through your user dashboard.

What is the "Kamustahan" feature?

This is a post-adoption monitoring module where adopters are encouraged to post periodic updates about their pet’s condition. It allows the organization to ensure the long-term well-being and successful integration of the animal into its new home.

Donations & Accountability

How can I send a donation?

The system features a QR Code-Based Donation Processing Module. You can use external payment platforms like GCash to send your contribution, which is then recorded in the system for tracking.

How do I know my donation is being used properly?

One of the system's primary goals is to enhance transparency and accountability. It uses Descriptive Analytics to summarize donor participation and resource allocation, allowing you to see the impact of your contributions.

Can I donate items instead of money?

While the system focuses on digital tracking of monetary support, organizations often accept "in-kind" donations. Please use the Contact Us page to coordinate the drop-off of food, medicine, or other supplies.

Technical & Security

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. The system undergoes Security and Usability Testing to identify and address vulnerabilities, ensuring that your personal records and application data are protected.

What if I don't have a stable internet connection?

The system is a web-based platform and requires stable internet access to operate effectively and ensure real-time updates for adoption and donation records.

How do I track my application status?

Once you have submitted your digital application, you can log in to your account to view live updates provided by the organization's officers and administrators.
