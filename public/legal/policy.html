Privacy Policy

Thanks for using our platform! Our Privacy Policy details how we handle your info. By using our system, you agree to these practices.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information: When you create an account to adopt or donate, we collect details like your name, contact information, and address to process your digital application.

Adopter Preferences: We collect specific data regarding the type of animal, sex, color, behavior, and age you prefer to provide accurate matchmaking rankings.

Matchmaking Inputs: We store the text descriptions you provide about your ideal pet, which are processed using Natural Language Processing (NLP) to improve compatibility results.

Donation Records: We record transaction details after confirmation from external platforms (like GCash) to ensure transparency and accountability.

Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the analytics dashboard, frequency of use, and any system errors to help us improve operational efficiency.

How We Use Your Information

Matchmaking & Analytics: We utilize rule-based weighted similarity scoring and NLP to match adopters with suitable animals and summarize adoption trends.

Monitoring Well-being: Your "Kamustahan" updates are used by administrators to monitor the post-adoption condition of animals every semester.

Operational Efficiency: Data is used to generate descriptive analytics reports to support data-driven decision-making for animal welfare organizations.

Information Sharing

Organization Access: Your data is shared only with the participating animal welfare organizations and administrators through role-based access control.

Third-Party Platforms: We do not process monetary transactions directly; we only record confirmed donations from external payment providers.

Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law to promote humane treatment and responsible ownership practices.

Data Security

System Integrity: We implement security and usability testing to address vulnerabilities and ensure the reliability of the platform.

Access Control: We use role-based management to ensure that sensitive animal records and adopter information are only accessible to authorized officers.

Your Choices

Update Information: You can review and update your digital adoption application or animal profiling records through your user account.

Monitoring Participation: You can provide regular updates through the "Kamustahan" module to stay engaged with the organization.

Children’s Privacy

Our platform is designed for responsible pet adoption and donation management. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18 without parental consent, as final adoption involves legal documentation and background checks.
