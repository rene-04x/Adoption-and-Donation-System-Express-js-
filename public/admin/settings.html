General Settings

Manage the basic information and branding of the organization, including website name, contact details, logo, theme color, and footer content.

About & Content Management

Edit and update the website's main content such as About Us, Mission, Vision, History, Developer section, and other public information displayed on the site.

Officer Management

Add, update, or remove organization officers including their photos, positions, term year, and short descriptions.

Donation Settings

Configure donation details such as GCash, bank information, QR codes, donation categories, and visibility of donation history.

User & Admin Management

Manage admin accounts, assign roles and permissions, reset passwords, and control access levels within the system.

Security Settings

Protect the system by updating passwords, enabling security features, managing backups, and restoring database data when needed.

Notification Settings

Set up automated notifications and email alerts for new donations, contact form submissions, and system updates.
