Frequently Asked Questions

How do I approve an Adoption application?
Navigate to the Adoptions module, select a "Pending" application, review the adopter's details, and click the "Approve" button. The animal's status will automatically update to "Adopted."
How do I update an animal's medical record?
Go to the Animals module and click on the specific animal's profile. You can edit their profile to update health status, vaccination dates, and behavioral notes.
How are QR Code donations verified?
Once a donor scans the QR code and sends funds, the transaction will appear in the Donations module. You can cross-reference the reference number and mark it as "Verified."
Can I export the monthly analytics reports?
Yes. In the Analytics module, you can view trends for adoptions and donations. Use the "Export" button at the top right to download a summary for your shelter's monthly report.
How do I update my Admin profile or password?
To edit your account details, navigate to the Settings module. Under the Profile tab, you can update your email, display name, and profile picture. To change your password, use the "Security" section to ensure your administrative access remains secure.
