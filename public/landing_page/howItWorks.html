Account

Find your perfect paws in a few simple steps when you Match, Meet, and Adopt.

Step 1: Create Your Adopter Profile

Start by signing up and filling out your Adopter Persona. Tell us about your living situation, your experience with pets, and your daily lifestyle. This helps our system understand the environment the pet will be moving into.

Step 2: Choose Your "Dream Pet"

Use our Matchmaking Feature to find your ideal companion.


  • Filter by: Type, Sex, Color, Age, and Behavior.

  • Behavioral Matching: Whether you want a high-energy running buddy or a calm “couch potato,” we connect you with pets that suit your lifestyle and preferences.

Step 3: Browse Your Top Matches

View your personalized list of recommendations. Each pet profile includes detailed health records, behavioral notes from our CSPC volunteers, and a Compatibility Score showing how well they fit your lifestyle.

Step 4: Submit a Digital Application

Once you find a match, click "Apply for Adoption." Your application, along with your profile data, is sent directly to our administrators for review. No more messy paperwork—track your application status right from your dashboard.

Step 5: Screening & Meet-and-Greet

If your application is shortlisted, our officers will contact you for a digital or physical screening. After initial approval, we will schedule a Meet-and-Greet at the CSPC facility or the volunteer’s location to ensure the chemistry is right.

Step 6: Welcome Home!

Once the final paperwork is signed and the orientation is complete, you are ready to take your new best friend home! Since PAWSsion is dedicated to the welfare of animals within the CSPC community, there are no adoption fees required. Our primary goal is ensuring every pet finds a loving, permanent home.
